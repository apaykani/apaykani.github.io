The Horizon Europe MSCA Doctoral Network **ASCEND** — *Next-Generation Accelerated and Scalable Modelling Frameworks for Chemically Reactive Flows* — has been funded, with a consortium total of €3.7M. QMUL coordinates the network and leads the scientific machine learning work package. Recruitment for doctoral candidates will open in due course.
