### Enquiries

**Prospective PhD students and postdocs** — please read [Join Us]({{ '/join/' | relative_url }}) first. Applications that address a specific research theme are far more likely to receive a reply.

**Media and industry** — email directly with a brief outline of what you need and your deadline.

**Collaboration** — I welcome enquiries from groups working in reactive flow modelling, scientific machine learning, and electrified powertrain thermal management.
